Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 Aug 2025

SpellRightPro (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we handle it.

Information we collect

  • Account info: Email address and authentication data via Firebase.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics (e.g., pages visited, device info) to improve the product.
  • Payment data: Managed by Stripe. We do not store full card details on our servers.
  • Content you provide: Word lists you upload or type for practice.

How we use data

  • Provide and improve SpellRightPro features.
  • Authenticate users and manage subscriptions.
  • Detect abuse and keep the service secure.
  • Communicate service updates and support responses.

Data processors

We use reputable third-party processors, including:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics)
  • Stripe (payments & subscriptions)
  • Netlify (hosting)
  • Google AdSense (ads on freemium pages only)

Retention

We retain account and subscription records for as long as your account is active and as required by law. You may request deletion of your account data (subject to legal/contractual limits).

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your choices

  • Access/update your account information in the app.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

Children’s privacy

SpellRightPro is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided us personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We’ll post the new version here and update the “Last updated” date.
