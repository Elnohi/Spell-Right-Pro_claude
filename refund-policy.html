How It Works

SpellRightPro Premium is a recurring subscription — monthly (CAD $5/month) or annual (CAD $45/year). You may cancel at any time through your Stripe billing portal or by contacting us.

When you cancel:

  • Your premium access continues until the end of your current billing period — you will not lose access immediately.
  • Your subscription will not renew after that date. No further charges are made.
  • Your practice history and progress data are retained so you can pick up where you left off if you resubscribe.
  • The remaining time on your billing period is not refunded — you keep access to everything you paid for until it expires.

Refund Policy

All subscription payments are non-refundable. When you subscribe, you pay for a defined billing period (monthly or annual) and you receive full premium access for that entire period.

  • Monthly subscriptions — the monthly charge is non-refundable. Cancel before the next renewal date to avoid being charged again.
  • Annual subscriptions — the annual charge is non-refundable. You retain access for the full 12 months after payment.
  • Partial-period refunds — we do not issue pro-rated refunds for unused time within a paid billing period.

We encourage you to try the free version of SpellRightPro before subscribing — all three modules (School, OET and Spelling Bee) are available at no cost so you can be confident the app meets your needs.

Billing Errors

If you believe you were charged in error — for example, a duplicate charge, a charge after cancellation, or a technical billing failure — please contact us at spellrightpro@gmail.com immediately.

Verified billing errors will be corrected and refunded in full regardless of the above policy. We investigate all such requests promptly and aim to resolve them within 3 business days.

How to Cancel

Cancelling takes less than 2 minutes:

1
Via the Stripe portal

Email spellrightpro@gmail.com and we will send you a link to your personal billing portal where you can cancel in one click.

2
Direct cancellation request

Email us with the subject line "Cancel my subscription" and include the email address you subscribed with. We will cancel it within 24 hours and confirm by email.

3
Confirmation

You will receive a cancellation confirmation by email. Your premium access continues until the expiry date shown in the confirmation.
