Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes — you can cancel any time from your account, or by emailing spellrightpro@gmail.com. Your premium access continues until the end of the period you've already paid for. After that, you simply revert to the free version automatically — no further charges, no surprises.
Is there a free trial?
We don't offer a free trial — you only pay when you choose to subscribe, and you can cancel anytime. Your premium access continues until the end of the period you've paid for. To try the app first, our free modules at freemium-oet, freemium-school and freemium-bee are free forever with no signup needed.
Can I switch between plans?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Do you offer educational discounts?
Yes! We offer special pricing for schools and educational institutions. Contact our sales team at spellrightpro@gmail.com for more information.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. All payments are processed securely with 256-bit SSL encryption.
